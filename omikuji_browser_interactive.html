総体運（そうたいうん）— Overall fortune

1
。

Translation: You are a steady, dependable person who climbs the stairs of life one sure step at a time.

Commentary: 階段を上がる is a metaphor for gradual progress. 一歩一歩 and 着実に emphasize patience, consistency, and reliability.

2
、、。

Translation: You can discern how things truly work, and because you are warm and compassionate, you are admired and loved by everyone.

Commentary: ものの道理 means the underlying logic or proper way of things. 人情に厚い describes someone warm-hearted, loyal, and considerate. 慕われます is passive: “to be admired or cherished.”

3
、、、、。

Translation: Moreover, even without taking the initiative yourself, people around you may naturally recommend you and offer their cooperation, giving you the potential to accomplish a major undertaking.

Commentary: 推挙される means being recommended or nominated because others recognize your qualities. 一大事業 means a major project or great undertaking. 成しとげる stresses carrying something difficult through to completion.
